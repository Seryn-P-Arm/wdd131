The Art Den - Site Plan

Site Name

Name: Seryn's Art Commissions

Reason: This name is explanitory yet it is playful and fun in tone and creativity. I want my visitors to be comfotable and enjoy the art shown and created.

Site Purpose

This site will provide a fun and easy way to order commissions for custom art as well as viewing the art of the creator in an art gallery, and then also contact the creator directly in the contact page. When ordering it will provide a list of aspects to consider when ordering one, about the process of the art creation so that visitors are informed about it and are aware of the factors of cost and price as well as time.

Scenarios

  • How can I commission a custom artwork?
  • Where can I see examples of past commissions and pricing details?
  • Where can I easily get in contact with an artist for an order without messaging from instagram or whatsapp where I could be missed?

Color Schema

  • #FF9F80 (Light Salmon): Used for header and footer background.
  • #D00000 (Red): Used for buttons and flower icons.
  • #FFE1D6 (Pippin): Used for paragragh backgrounds.
  • #FFFC31 (Yellow): Used for button hovers.

Typography

  • Fascinate Inline: Used for headings.
  • Eagle Lake: Used for body text.
